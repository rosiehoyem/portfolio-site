Latest Side Projects3

Our City

Our City is an on-going initiative that aims to facilitate transparency into the governing process of the Minneapolis City Council and increase public engagement through greater access to decision makers and a better understanding of decision making processes. The project got it's start in the summer of 2014 with the CURA:Tech program, and has continued as a side-project of mine and through efforts of other members of Open Twin Cities

Learn more about this project on this site which gathers together a of the different prototypes that have come from this initiative.
